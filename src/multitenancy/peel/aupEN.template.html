CONSENT TO DISCLOSE YOUR PERSONAL HEALTH INFORMATION

By submitting your information through the ICON website, you are consenting to the use and disclosure of personal health information as follows:

Peel Public Health will upload the information into a secure provincial immunization database. Once uploaded, the information may be shared with or accessed by other health care providers if they administer immunizations or are required to maintain a record of immunization for the individual.

This information will be used by Peel Public Health, or an Ontario public health unit where the individual resides, for the purposes of administration and evaluation of the immunization and vaccine preventable disease programs.

If you do not want this information shared, please provide notification to the following address:

You may access and use this website solely for the purposes of submitting and/or retrieving your own immunization records, or for submitting and retrieving immunization records of a dependent. You warrant that the information or material that you submit to and/or retrieve from the website through your access to, or use of, the website does not infringe the rights of any person or entity.

SECURITY

You are responsible for the security of the device, browser, and network that you are using to access this website. You will use reasonable care to safeguard your Ontario Immunization ID and PIN to access the website. You will not reveal your Ontario Immunization ID or your PIN to anyone.
